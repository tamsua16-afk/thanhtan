Our Advantages選ばれる理由

Why Choose Thanh Tan?なぜタントゥアンなのか？

Five compelling reasons why leading manufacturers choose Thanh Tan Industrial Cluster as their base of operations. 世界の製造業リーダーがタントゥアン工業団地を選ぶ5つの理由。

📍

Strategic Location戦略的立地

Direct access to CT08 Expressway connecting Ninh Binh – Hai Phong and convenient access to Hanoi. Premium corner position at CT08 & CT16 intersection. CT08高速道路に直結し、ニンビン〜ハイフォンを結ぶ。CT08とCT16の交差点という最良の角地に位置。

🚛

Logistics Advantage物流優位性

Fast connectivity to seaports, airports and major industrial corridors in Northern Vietnam. Direct CT08 access minimizes transport time and costs. 港湾・空港・ベトナム北部の主要工業回廊へ迅速アクセス。CT08直結により輸送時間とコストを最小化。

👷

Competitive Workforce豊富な労働力

Over 30,000 working-age residents within the surrounding labor catchment area. Abundant, cost-competitive labor pool ready for recruitment. 周辺地域の労働力人口3万人以上。豊富で競争力のある人件費の労働力がすぐに採用可能。

⚡

Ready Infrastructure完備されたインフラ

Power, water, fiber-optic telecommunications and centralized wastewater treatment system. All utilities in place for immediate operations. 電力・水道・光ファイバー通信・集中廃水処理設備が整備済み。即座に操業開始できる環境を提供。

🤝

Investor Support投資家支援

Full investment procedure assistance, recruitment support and project implementation guidance. Comprehensive legal and financial services. 投資手続き・採用支援・プロジェクト実施まで全面サポート。法律・金融サービスも完備。

🌱

Future-Ready Expansion将来の拡張性

Room for future expansion with reserved land zones. Green belts and open spaces for a sustainable, modern industrial environment. 将来の拡張用地を確保済み。緑地帯と開放空間を備えた持続可能な近代工業環境。
